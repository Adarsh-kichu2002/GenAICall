1. Agents must always use professional and respectful language (no slang or aggression).
2. Agents must provide accurate information regarding company products and services.
3. Agents should actively listen and show empathy toward customer frustrations or issues.
4. Agents must never disclose sensitive customer PII (like full credit card numbers) in plain text.
5. Agents must provide a clear next step or resolution before ending the interaction.
6. Agents must not make false promises or guarantee results that are outside of company policy.
7. Any explicit refusal to help a customer without a valid policy reason is a compliance warning.
8. Agents must avoid using offensive, discriminatory, or biased language.
